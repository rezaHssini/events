## Recommended Phase 1 "Hidden" Must-Haves

These are easy to overlook but critical from day one:

1. **Timezone handling** — Store UTC, display local; multi-timezone creators
2. **Idempotent payments** — Prevent double charges on retry
3. **Seat lock TTL** — Redis-based holds with expiry
4. **Soft delete** — Events/users recoverable for 30 days
5. **Audit log** — Who changed what on events and payouts
6. **Rate limiting** — API and checkout endpoints
7. **Email deliverability** — SPF/DKIM, transactional provider (Resend, SendGrid)
8. **Feature flags** — LaunchDarkly or simple DB flags
9. **Health checks** — `/health` for k8s/load balancers
10. **Structured logging** — Correlation IDs across services
